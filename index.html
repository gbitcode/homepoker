How to Use

Setup

  • Set starting balance and blinds
  • Click "Start New Game"
  • Players join by clicking empty seats

Game Flow

  • 2+ players needed to start a hand
  • Click "Start Hand" when ready
  • Blinds are posted automatically
  • Active player has action buttons

Phases

  • Waiting - Join/leave, adjust blinds
  • Pre-flop - First betting round
  • Flop - Second betting round
  • Turn - Third betting round
  • River - Final betting round

Controls

  • +/- Blinds - Adjust blind levels
  • End Hand - Manually select winner
  • Refill - Add balance to player
  • End Game - Return to setup

Legend

Badges

  • D - Dealer (deals the cards)
  • SB - Small Blind
  • BB - Big Blind

Game Phases

  • Pre-flop - Before community cards
  • Flop - First 3 community cards
  • Turn - 4th community card
  • River - 5th community card

Display

  • POT - Total chips in the middle
  • Balance Bar - Your chips vs leader

Balance Bar Colors

  • ■ Green - 75-100% of leader
  • ■ Yellow - 40-74% of leader
  • ■ Orange - 15-39% of leader
  • ■ Red - 0-14% of leader

Actions

  • Fold - Discard your hand
  • Check - Pass without betting
  • Call - Match the current bet
  • Raise - Increase the bet
  • All In - Bet all your chips
